# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in CadOwl a respectful, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of role, experience level, or background.

## Standards

### Positive Behavior
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the project
- Show empathy toward other contributors

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Harassment of any kind (public or private)
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Trolling, name-calling, or sustained disruption
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- Code, issues, PRs, commits in this repository
- Discussions in `#mint-support` Slack
- Walmart-internal meetings about CadOwl
- Any other channels related to this project

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the repository maintainer (see [CODEOWNERS](.github/CODEOWNERS))
or escalated through Walmart's standard HR/People channels.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Consequences may include:
- Private warning
- Public apology required
- Temporary ban from contributing
- Permanent ban from the project
- Escalation to Walmart HR
