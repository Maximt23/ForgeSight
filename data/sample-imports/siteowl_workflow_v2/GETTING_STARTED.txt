========================================
      GETTING STARTED IN 3 STEPS
========================================

STEP 1: Open the Tool
---------------------
Double-click on:  SiteOwl_Workflow.html

The tool opens in your web browser.


STEP 2: Load Your ASDPX File
----------------------------
1. In Step 1 (Import), drag your .asdpx file onto the drop zone
   OR click "Browse Files" and select your file

2. The tool will automatically:
   ✓ Parse the ASDPX (JSON format)
   ✓ Extract all devices with GPS coordinates
   ✓ Convert GPS → pixel coordinates → feet → display units
   ✓ Map part numbers to camera models
   ✓ Assign SiteOwl-approved Device Types
   ✓ Calculate coverage cones
   ✓ Generate 56-column CSV

3. Click "Continue to Preview"


STEP 3: Review and Export
-------------------------
In Step 2 (Preview):
- Verify building dimensions are correct
- Check that Field Notes column shows GPS coordinates
- Scroll right → to see all 56 columns
- Click "Launch Floorplan Editor" (optional)

In Step 3 (Floorplan Editor) [Optional]:
- Drag devices to adjust positions
- Toggle coverage cones (eye button)
- Toggle floorplan image (building button)
- View pricing in right sidebar
- Delete devices with × button
- Click "Export CSV" when done

Or in Step 2:
- Click "Download CSV" to save immediately


YOUR OUTPUT FILE
----------------
The exported CSV is ready to import into SiteOwl!

File format: siteowl_import.csv
56 columns matching SiteOwl standard
